We sell & buy Cars

If you want to buy a used car, just tell us what you want: whether with navigation system, leather seats, rear-view camera, low mileage or any special equipment - we are looking for it for you at any car manufacturer. If you would like to buy a company car, or are also a car dealer, or are looking for a special vehicle - we would be happy to support you here. And the positive thing is: we have all vehicles checked so that you can be sure that your car is in good condition.

About Us

We have been in Hamburg since May 1998. You will find us in Hamburg-Niendorf near the airport, we can be reached quickly via the (exit no. 24 Schnelsen / Niendorf). Our competent team is available to answer your questions at any time and will be happy to provide information about our range of vehicles and our services. You will always find new used car offers from all brands, especially MB, PORSCHE, BMW, AUDI and VW.
